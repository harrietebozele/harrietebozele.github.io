About

Hi, my name is Harriet and I am an entry level full stack software developer about to obtain a certificate in full stack development from the MITxPro Full Stack Software Development program. I am versed in the MERN stack which majority of my full stack projects are based on. I have knowledge of the use of languages and applications such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS, ReactJS, Bootstrap, NPM, YARN, NodeJS, ExpressJS, GraphQL, NextJS, MongoDB and authentication systems such as Firebase.

I have worked on various projects which are included in the drop down menu on this website, and many more on my github repository whose link is also included in the drop down menu. Updates would be made regularly to this site to include projects that I will have worked on in the future so feel free to check out this website regularly to check for new changes. Also, feel free to reach out to me if you do have any questions, comments or concerns about anything on this website!
